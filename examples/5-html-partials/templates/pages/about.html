Variable Substitution

Templates use a simple {{variable}} syntax for dynamic content: 

<h1>{{site_name}}</h1>
<p>{{tagline}}</p>

These are replaced at runtime by the PHP script, allowing for dynamic values while keeping templates pure HTML.
